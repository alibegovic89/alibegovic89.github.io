Frequently asked questions

Is this investment advice?
No. Depot‑Check is an analysis and education tool: it shows you facts, metrics and risks in your portfolio — overlaps, costs, concentration, currencies. It does not tell you what to buy or sell, and it is not investment advice within the meaning of German securities law (WpHG). The decisions stay yours.
Which brokers and formats are supported?
At launch: CSV and PDF statements from Trade Republic, Scalable Capital, ING, comdirect and Interactive Brokers — plus manual entry for everything else. Your broker missing? Tell us; the list grows with demand. And if an import doesn't parse cleanly for you, we check it by hand before your report goes out.
What happens to my data?
Your position list is used only for your report, processed GDPR-compliantly on EU servers, and permanently deleted on request, instantly. No broker login, no bank API, no data selling — ever.
Does it cover crypto and individual stocks?
Yes. The check covers ETFs, individual stocks, funds and crypto — and the interplay between them (e.g. a Bitcoin position on top of tech-heavy ETFs) is often the most interesting part of the analysis.
What if the report tells me nothing new?
14-day money-back, no questions asked. If your report doesn't give you a single aha moment, you get the full price back — one short email is enough.
Isn't this just generic AI waffle?
That's exactly what we refuse to ship — there are enough vague "diversify more" tips out there. Every finding names specific positions, percentages and euro amounts from your portfolio (see the sample extract above), and every report is reviewed by a human before it's sent.
