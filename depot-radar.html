Frequently asked questions

Is this investment advice?
No. Depot‑Radar is an information and education service: we summarise what happened and explain why it's relevant for long-term investors — without buy or sell recommendations, and without investment advice within the meaning of German securities law (WpHG). The decisions stay yours.
Which holdings are covered?
Stocks, ETFs and crypto on all major exchanges. For ETFs we also watch the top constituents: if Microsoft reports earnings and your world ETF is 5 % Microsoft, you'll know. Something missing? Tell us — coverage grows with demand.
Do I need an account with a specific broker?
No. Because Depot‑Radar needs no account access, it's completely broker-independent — Trade Republic, Scalable Capital, ING, Interactive Brokers, your local bank or a brokerage abroad: you enter your holdings once, done.
How many emails will I get?
One per week — on Sundays. Plus an optional short instant mail for truly major events on your holdings (profit warning, dividend cut, takeover bid), which you can switch on or off. No daily nagging, promised.
What happens to my data?
Your holdings list and email address are used only for your Radar mail, GDPR-compliant on EU servers. No selling, no sharing, one-click deletion.
How fast can I cancel?
With one click in every mail — no notice period, no forms, no "please call us". Paying monthly means being free monthly. And the first Radar mail is free anyway.
