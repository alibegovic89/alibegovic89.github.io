Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?
Yes. You get the full planner, forever, including future versions. The "price" is your email address — that's how I let you know when I ship new tools for EU investors. One-click unsubscribe, and you keep the file.
Excel, Google Sheets or LibreOffice?
All three. The planner uses only standard formulas (SUMIFS — no macros, no array magic), so it opens cleanly everywhere. For Google Sheets: upload the file to Drive and open with Sheets.
What is NEEDS / WANTS / KEEP?
Every euro gets one of three jobs: NEEDS (must pay: rent, groceries, insurance), WANTS (choose to pay: travel, eating out) and KEEP (pay yourself: emergency fund, ETF savings plan, pension). The classic 50/30/20 rule is the compass; the dashboard shows where you actually stand.
I don't live in Germany — does it still work?
Absolutely. The German categories (Nebenkosten, broadcast fee, Deutschlandticket) are just pre-filled rows — rename any of them and the maths doesn't change. The system is universal.
What happens to my email?
It goes on my list, GDPR-compliant, and is never sold or shared. You'll get the planner and occasional mails about tools I'm building (like the Depot-Check below). Unsubscribe = one click.
